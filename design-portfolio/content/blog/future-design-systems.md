Design systems are evolving rapidly. With the advent of AI, we are seeing a shift from static component libraries to dynamic, generative design systems that can adapt to user needs in real-time.

## The Role of AI

AI can help automate the creation of variations, test accessibility, and even generate code from design tokens.
